Chocolate Milkshake

Description

Chocolate Milkshake is a very popular milk based sweet beverage that is enojoyed throughout the world.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups premium quality chocolate ice cream
  • 2 (1.65 ounce) bars dark chocolate candy bars, diced
  • Â½ cup whipped cream
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate shavings, for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine ice cream, diced chocolate, and 1/2 cup whipped cream in a blender. Blend until smooth
  2. Pour into 2 glasses; garnish with chocolate shavings and 1/4 cup whipped cream
