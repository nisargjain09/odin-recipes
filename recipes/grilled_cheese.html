Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled Cheese (short for grilled cheese sandwich) is a popular snack which is very easy and quick to prepare and enjoyed in most part of the western world.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of bread of choice (sandwich, brown, whole wheat, etc.)
  • Butter
  • 2 Cheese slices

Steps

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up)
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted
